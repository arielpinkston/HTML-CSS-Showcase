Chocolate

There are so many packaged foods in circulation that choosing just one is difficult. However, I think one of my favorites has to be chocolate. It's a wonderful ingredient in other recipes, it's delicious on its own, and it comes in a convenient package. While there are three kinds of chocolate

  • White
  • Dark
  • Milk

my favorite kind is dark. The extra cocoa powder truly brings out the best flavors of the chocolate.

According to the BA test kitchen, Guittard Cocoa Rouge is the best cocoa powder of them all.
