Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup white sugar
  1. Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake in the pre-heated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
  5. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely. Store in an airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!
